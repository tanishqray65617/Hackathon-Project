About GDSC

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) is a community of students passionate about Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome to join.

Our GDSC chapter aims to help students bridge the gap between theory and practice by providing workshops, events, and project opportunities in various domains such as mobile development, web development, cloud computing, machine learning, and more.

By joining GDSC, members get access to Google Developer resources, workshops led by experts, peer-to-peer learning opportunities, and the chance to work on projects with real-world impact. We believe in fostering an inclusive environment where students can grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning setting and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
