About Ukriti

Ukriti is the entrepreneurship club at our college, fostering innovation and startup development among students.

We provide mentorship, resources, and networking opportunities to help students turn their business ideas into successful ventures.

Our upcoming Shark Tank-style competition will allow students to pitch their startups to 50+ investment partners for real funding.
